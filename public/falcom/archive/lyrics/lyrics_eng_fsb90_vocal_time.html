Beyond a Far-Off Time (Sorcerian - Ending I)

Music: Sound Team JDK
Arrangement: Hiroshi Arakawa
Lyric: Yoshihiko Andoh
Vocal: Charlite

Japanese Lyric

Hateshi nai toki o koeru
Tabi no hajimari
Itsu made mo samenai
Yume o misete

We begin a journey
Whose end lies beyond a far-off time.
Show me a dream
From which I shall never awaken.

Aa mou ichido ano koro no
Jiyuu na kokoro de
Saa hitomi o tojite
Kaze no uta o kuchizusamu

Ah, once again, with a heart
As free as in the old days,
Close your eyes
And sing to yourself a song of the wind.

Kizutsukitaoreru yoru mo
Itsuka wa owaru
Kanashimi mo fushigi ni
Wasurete yuku

Even when you are hurt and fallen in the darkness,
The night will end soon,
And sadness as well
Will miraculously be forgotten.

Aa sayonara ga ienakute
Sora o miagetara
Mou yoake ga chikai
Asayake ni somaru kokoro

Ah...I can't say goodbye.
I lift my eyes to the sky,
And find that dawn already draws near,
Painting my heart with the light of the rising sun.

Yasashisa wa itsumo hito ni
Yuuki o kureru
Taisetsu na dareka o
Shinjiru toki

Kindness always gives courage to a person
When he believes in someone whom he loves.

Aa ai dake o sagashiteru
Nanimo osorenai
Saa atsui omoi o
Kokoro no naka ni kakushite

Ah...I am looking only for love,
And nothing gives me fear.
A burning passion
Hides deep within my heart.
