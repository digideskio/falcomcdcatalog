Japanese Lyric

Meguriaeru made yumemiru
Kono tozasareta yoru no naka de
Ai wa itsumo sono inochi o

Until we can meet again, I'll dream
Through these dark, lonely nights.
Whenever people throw away their lives,
They do so in search of love.

Sono ude no naka de
Subete wasurete nemureba dream forever
Kooritsuku yoru mo

If, in your arms, I could
Forget everything and sleep (Dream forever),
These frozen nights
Would one day pass beyond memory.

Ever since that time when our fingers first touched,
We have felt what the future holds.
For whose sake were we born into this world?
We came searching for someone to love.

Ima wa yasashiku tsutsunde
Ikusen no yoru o
Koete deatta futari

Take my wounded heart,
And wrap it in tenderness (Stay forever).
Two people, trascending
Thousands of nights to meet.

Kotoba sae wasure
Ai ni yurarete nemureba dream forever
Kooritsuku sekai
Itsuka anata ga kaeru

If I could forget even words,
And sleep, cradled by love (Dream forever),
You would one day
Change this frozen world.
